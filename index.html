Happy Day, Happy Juneteenth, Happy Freedom

“Happy Day, Happy Juneteenth, Happy Freedom” is a vibrant and heartfelt tribute to the joy, resilience, and history of Juneteenth. Through a simple yet powerful lens, this book celebrates the journey from bondage to liberation, honoring the voices of the past while inspiring new generations to embrace freedom, culture, and unity with pride and purpose.
